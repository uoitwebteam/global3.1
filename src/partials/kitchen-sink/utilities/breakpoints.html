Breakpoints

Breakpoints have been customized from default Foundation breakpoints. (In a future update, breakpoints may be simplified and consolidated.)

large 1200 - 1399 px

Note: "Mobile" view (mobile navigation, sidebar stack) is from xxsmall to small. "Desktop" view is medium and up.
